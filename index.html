Company Executive Summary

GC2 Associates are a team of highly experienced professionals from the security, military and IT sectors, providing specialist consultation, installation and professional technical support services to our clients.

The combination of the backgrounds from these sectors allow GC2 to consider our clients’ requirements from a new perspective, ensuring maximum value and effectiveness.

GC2 are independent and manufacturer-agnostic covering corporate, private, CNI, custodial and defence organisations.

Consultation

GC2 offer a wide range of consultancy support. From advice on threat analysis and appropriate physical and electronic countermeasure specification, design reviews, troubleshooting integration issues, or cyber security threat analysis, GC2 have the expertise to support clients and protect your interests.

Installation

GC2 Project Managers are qualified Prince 2 Practitioners, IOSH, SMSTS and First Aiders ensuring our project approach and project management standards align with best practice and we can demonstrate competence under Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2007 (CDM)

GC2 team members have been involved in the design, specification and installation of many of the UKs’ highest security and CNI sites. We are happy to discuss previous and future potential work in an appropriately “cleared” environment.

Factory Acceptance Testing

The GC2 secure facility has been specifically designed for show piecing integrated security systems with equipment pre-build workshops, server rooms, workshops and an upper level conference control room and conference room.

Our in-house engineers can assemble and test a wide range of equipment to ensure quality before deployment. A strictly managed procedure for pre-build and test documentation ensures that each unit is built to the same high standard of workmanship, quality and is rigorously tested before being packed for shipping.

All prebuild equipment is logged within our test procedures to capture make, model and serial numbers for control and tractability with a test certificate being produced to verify that the equipment / system has been fully tested. This process goes a long way to ensuring that the system will be installed and commissioned with minimal disruption to the site or customer.
